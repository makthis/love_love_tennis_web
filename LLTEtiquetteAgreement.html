Etiquette Agreement

Host:

Make sure to provide balls and water for your guests.

Guest:

Make sure to pay any guest fees that are required.  Adhere to parking and country club rules.

Time:

You must arrive 10 minutes before the match starts, on the court, ready to play.  (Please use the ladies BEFORE you go down to the court).

Dress:

Only ILMDP designs should be worn, whether one of our court-to-cocktails dresses or, a skirt and top, this is the uniform of the LOVE LOVE TENNIS league.

Attitude:

It's in the name, it's all about LOVE.  You must be kind, positive, uplifting, and full of LOVE LOVE.

Rules:

All of Adeline’s tournament rules must be followed - there are no exceptions.  A 15-minute warm-up is granted - but upon starting - it’s a 9 game set - change sides only once after 4 games - and then break and switch partner.  Write the love note.  Then repeat.

Mission:

To have an enriching tennis experience with three ladies as partners.  Learning to bring the best out of each partner whilst having fun.

Privacy:

Gossiping, or mean mouthing will not be tolerated.  Keep your match details confidential - unless it's empowering to share.

Protection:

At LOVE LOVE TENNIS we are not only protective of our member's health physically, but also mentally and emotionally too.  Negativity is unbecoming of a LOVE LOVE TENNIS lady - and membership will be revoked.

Love:

You have only truly won if each of your three partners leaves feeling LOVED.  Make the best memories by having sweet matches and making friends with everyone that you meet.
